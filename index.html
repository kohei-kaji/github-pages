Biography

Hi! I’m Kohei Kajikawa, a master’s student at the University of Tokyo.
My research interests include computational linguistics and computational psycholinguistics.

We can immediately understand the meaning of a sentence when we see or hear it.
I am interested in what kind of grammar is used and how the grammar is algorithmically employed unconsciously during this process.
Additionally, I am curious about why the grammar has taken its current form.

Interests
